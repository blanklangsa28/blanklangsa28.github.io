Provincial Rice Technology Forum 2025 (PRTF 2025)

The PRTF 2025 aims to gather rice technology experts, farmers, and stakeholders across the Philippines to share innovations, research, and strategies for a sustainable rice future. Key topics include:

  • Climate-resilient rice varieties
  • Smart farming technologies
  • Water management practices
  • Post-harvest innovations
  • Policy and funding opportunities

Join us in 2025 for a groundbreaking forum that will shape the future of rice farming in the Philippines!
